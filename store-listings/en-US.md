## Description
Voxis is a real-time voice translator for Windows. It listens to the sound playing on your PC — a video, a live stream, a game, or the other side of an online meeting — and plays back a spoken translation in your own language, while it's still being spoken. Not just subtitles: you actually hear it, live.

Like a human simultaneous interpreter, Voxis translates continuously and stays a few seconds behind, balancing speed and accuracy on its own.

WHAT YOU CAN DO
• Translate any video, stream, or movie into your language with live spoken audio
• Understand games, voice chat, and streamers in real time
• Follow online meetings and calls — translate the other person, and even your own voice, two-way
• Read live captions and keep a searchable transcript history (export to SRT / VTT / TXT)

WHY VOXIS
• 70+ languages supported
• Driverless system-audio capture — nothing extra to install for one-way translation
• Smart ducking lowers the original audio while the translation speaks, so you're never talked over
• Honest, low-latency design — no fake "instant," just a natural interpreter-style delay
• Privacy-first: your audio is streamed only to perform the translation and is not stored on our servers
• An open-source build with bring-your-own-key (BYOK) and zero telemetry is available on GitHub

POWERED BY AI
Translation and spoken output are produced by Google's Gemini live translation AI for natural, simultaneous speech-to-speech results.

GET STARTED
Install Voxis, sign in, pick your language, and press start — new accounts include free translation minutes. Upgrade anytime for more.

Voxis works with any app that plays sound on your PC. Your privacy policy and terms: https://voxislive.com/privacy · https://voxislive.com/terms
